## Product
Liminal — a futuristic prosthetic rehabilitation web platform with AR capabilities.

## Register
product (dashboards, patient programs, therapist tools — design SERVES the product, with a brand landing page as the entry point)

## Users
- **Patients in rehabilitation**: people with limb amputations following personalized prosthetic training programs, tracking recovery progress, calibrating devices via AR
- **Therapists**: licensed professionals managing patient caseloads, building remote training plans, reviewing outcomes data
- **Experience users**: people exploring prosthetic technology and AR interfaces without a clinical need

## Purpose
Give prosthetic rehabilitation a digital home — a platform where patients train with AR-guided calibration, therapists monitor remotely, and progress is measured with precision. The product makes an emotionally complex journey feel supported, capable, and even beautiful.

## Tone
Capable, precise, warm without being clinical. Never cold or institutional. Not playful — serious about function, but with a sense of possibility. Like a well-designed medical device: trustworthy and considered.

## Brand personality
Precise. Futuristic. Human.

## Anti-references
- Generic healthcare portals (generic blue, clinical grids)
- Purple UI (forbidden)
- Dark-mode-by-default "cool tool" aesthetic
- SaaS dashboard templates with hero metrics and gradient text
- Aggressive glassmorphism used decoratively everywhere

## Primary accent
Teal / cyan — oklch(0.62 0.19 195). Used for interactive elements, glows, highlights, progress indicators.

## Design direction
Light theme (white/off-white), futuristic but minimal, holographic aesthetic through restraint — soft teal glows, glass-morphism on navbar only, subtle mesh gradients in hero background. Spacious, precise, clean. Bilingual (Hebrew RTL default, English LTR).

## Accessibility
WCAG AA contrast minimum. RTL-complete layout mirroring for Hebrew. Reduced-motion alternatives for all animations.
